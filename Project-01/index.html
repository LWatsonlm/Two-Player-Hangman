Can you save your friend!?

Your best friend has been excused of being a witch! Help him escape death by hanging by guessing the right word!

Do you dare to play?

Player One: Enter your word!

Player two: Guess letter by letter

Instructions: This is a two player game. One person picks a word and then the other tries to guess it by making repeated guesses to letters in the word.

If the letter is not in the word then a body part will be added to your friend. Good luck!
